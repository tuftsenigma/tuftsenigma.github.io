Our Mission

Enigma is an independent publication based out of Tufts University focused on the crossroads of Computer Science, Data Science, and journalism. We examine and discuss cross-disciplinary themes within modern computing as well as use data-driven methods to visualize and analyze real world issues and ideas. Enigma truly strives to foster "journalism through a computational lens."
